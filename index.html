The Darwin Papers

Charles Robert Darwin FRS FRGS FLS FZS was an English naturalist whose scientific theory of evolution by natural selection became the foundation of modern evolutionary studies.

His journey on the HMS Beagle allowed him to observe various geological formations and fossils, leading to his monumental work, On the Origin of Species (1859).
