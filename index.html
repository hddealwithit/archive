The Darwin Digital Archive

Welcome to the digital repository of Charles Darwin's scientific manuscripts. This archive contains primary sources regarding the 1831-1836 voyage of the HMS Beagle.

Field Notes & Correspondence

Darwin's notes on the Galapagos finches provided the empirical evidence required to support the theory of natural selection. His work shifted the paradigm of biological sciences permanently.

  • Geological Observations (1844)
  • Monograph on Cirripedia (1851)
  • The Power of Movement in Plants (1880)
— Charles Robert Darwin
